The Healing for Home Project

Social Media Strategy & Impact Marketing Portfolio

Executive Summary

This project demonstrates my ability to lead a cause-driven marketing campaign for medical aid in Kosovë. My focus was on leveraging historical storytelling, emotional urgency, and visual transparency to build brand trust and drive community engagement.

STORYTELLING

Heritage & Trust

Utilized the legacy of figures like Queen Geraldine and Sofia Noti to build an emotional bridge between the cause and the audience, establishing long-term credibility.

AWARENESS

Urgency Marketing

Executed a high-impact awareness campaign using the hook: "What if your hospital ran out of supplies?" to create immediate psychological urgency and call-to-action.

TRANSPARENCY

On-Ground Logistics

Strategic use of real-time supply delivery content to showcase "Proof of Work," significantly increasing donor trust and social proof for the organization.
