Healing for Home

Bridging the Gap Between Care and Crisis in Kosovë

Who We Are

Healing for Home is a grassroots initiative sending urgently needed medical supplies to clinics and hospitals in underfunded communities across Kosovë. We bridge the gap between care and crisis.

Imagine having to drive over an hour to get treated because your local hospital doesn't have the supplies to help you. For too many in Kosovë, this is reality.

Our Recent Impact in Lipjan

Life-Saving Equipment

Purchased a respirator and an EKG machine for the Main Family Health Center.

Emergency Kits

Provided 4 fully stocked emergency trauma kits to a facility that previously had no access to these tools.

Stability in Crisis

These supplies represent stability, a better response to trauma, and dignity in care.
